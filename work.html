Waste Collection

It is the transfer of solid waste from the point of use and disposal to the point of treatment or landfill. Waste collection also includes the curbside collection of recyclable materials that technically are not waste, as part of a municipal landfill diversion program.

STANDARD REQUIREMENTS FOR APPLICATION FOR PSP IN LAGOS STATE

  • Evidence of registration with CAC (Certificate of Incorporation)
  • Evidence of registration with PPA
  • Evidence of completion of course at Lawma Academy
  • Evidence of acquisition of minimum of 3 compactors of minimum 20 cubic metric volume capacity each
  • Certificate of Vehicle Inspection
  • Sketch map of slot
  • Operational office within the slot

STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURES (S.O.P)

  • All tenements must be serviced at least twice weekly
  • Evidence of service inform of logbook must be provided at all times
  • Low collection coverage and irregular collection frequencies if found out, attract stringed penalty
  • All collection staff must be properly kitted and sitting on top of collection vehicle will also attract penalty
  • Collection truck must patiently wait to collect wastes from tenements
  • Proper route plan for waste collection should be submitted and strictly adhered to.
